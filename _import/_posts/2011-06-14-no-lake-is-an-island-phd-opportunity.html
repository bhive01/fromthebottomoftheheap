UKLEON is led by Ian Jones at CEH Lancaster. A fully-funded PhD project is associated with the UKLEON project, with the aim of investigating high resolution coherence in lake dynamics. The successful student will be based at CEH Lancaster but registered for a PhD in the Department of Geography at UCL, receiving co-supervision via both institutions. The project would suit a computer literate and numerate student, with a background in limnology/ecology/environmental science and some statistical experience an advantage, but full training will be provided during the PhD. The closing date for applications is 29 July 2011. See the UCL advert for full details of the PhD project and the application procedure. If you are interested in the PhD you can contact me or the other supervisors for informal discussions about the project.
